## Abilities

- **Fine Beam (Front)** - □ □ □ □

  - *An razor thin laser that carves very small cavities that are difficult to find for repair.*
  - Damage: 3d6 Energy
  - Range: 5

- **Psionic Siphon** - □ □ □ □ □ □

  - *Magnetic coils that sap mental power.*
  - Damage: 2d8 Energy
  - Range: 6
  - When this system is used against a drone that has at least one Drift die stored, it may capture one Drift die and hold it hostage. Take a stored Drift die and place it on this system. The Drift Die remains on this system until this Wraith takes damage, at which point it is returned to the Drifter that originally assigned it. This system can hold a maximum of 4 Drift Dice.
