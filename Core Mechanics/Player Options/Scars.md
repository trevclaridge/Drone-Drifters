# Scars

**Agoraphobic**: The vastness of space leaves you trembling. When you start a round piloting a drone that has no other combatants within 6 hexes, you roll with 2 less Drift Dice than your standard pool.

---

**Introvert**: You do your best work in the silence of being alone. When you begin a round sharing a drone with at least one other Drifter consciousnesses, roll with 2 less Drift Dice than your current standard pool.

---

**Restless**: You feel in innate need to find and feel new things. You must take the free launch at the end of your turn, and it must be to a drone of a different class than your current drone's class.

---

**Sensitive Synapses**: Your connection with your drone runs deepâ€”maybe too deep. When the drone you are piloting takes Energy damage, half of the damage that is not blocked by Shields is applied directly to you (rounded up).

---

**Claustrophobic**: You need room to truly unleash your piloting skills. When you start a round piloting a drone that is within 6 hexes of another combatant, you roll with 2 less Drift dice than your standard pool.

---

**Overindulgent**: You get carried away, you can't help yourself. When you piloting a drone that deals the damage that wrecks a combatant, roll a d6. If the value is a 1 or a 2, the wreck is destroyed and does not yield any Credits when the battle is finished.

---

**Blood Lust**: You have a deep love and need for destruction. If you end your turn without having done at least 1 point of damage (of any type), you roll with 2 less Drift Dice than your current standard pool in the next round.

---

**Black Sheep**: You just don't quite fit in with the rest of the crew. You do not gain a Cohesion when you Assist another Drifter.

---

**Sullen**: You can't bring yourself to enjoy this thing we call war. When a Cohesion is being awarded for an enemy combatant being wrecked, roll a d6. If the value is a 1 or a 2, you do not get the Cohesion.

---

**Squeamish**: The thrill of battle is a foreign concept to you. If a combatant is wrecked within 6 hexes of the drone you are piloting, you roll with 2 less Drift Dice than your current standard pool in the next round.
