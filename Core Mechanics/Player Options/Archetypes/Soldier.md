What are you ordered to do? Whatever it is, you're not happy about it, but you know your duty, so you get to work without complaint. **+6 Credits**

**Know the Nuance (6 Stress).** You learn what you were *really* doing. The task you performed has an unexpected positive effect on the fleet. What are is it? How does this reframe how you feel about orders? **+3 Cohesion**

**Not Always On Your Side (2 Cohesion).** Someone above you in the chain of command takes advantage of your loyalty. It rocks you. **+5 Stress**
