**Corpo Torpedo™** - □ □ □ □ □

*A multi-tiered missile that eats money.*

- Activation Cost: 6
- Activation Cost Floor: 4
- Credit Cost: 9
- Damage: 1d6+dX Ballistic
- Range: 6
- Keywords: Weapon
- Facing: Front
- When a Drifter actives this system, they can choose to spend a number of Credits to temporarily increase the tier of their subscription license to the torpedo manufacturing service, unlocking the high-level explosive features of their Corpo Torpedo™. For every two Credits spent, add an additional d6 to the activation's damage role. The Drifter must declare how many Credits they are spending before rolling for damage.

- Activation Cost: 5
- Activation Cost Floor: 3
- Credit Cost: 9
- Damage: 2d6+dX Ballistic
- Range: 5
- Keywords: Weapon
- Facing: Sides
- The projectiles of this weapon can target enemies 1 hex to either side of the standard cone of side-fire weaponry.

**Reinforced Plating** - □ □ □

*Strong metal alloy welded over areas of this drone often exposed to damage.*

- Credit Cost: 5
- Keywords: Upgrade
- While this system is operable, this drone has +6 Armor.

---

**Pulse Strengtheners** - □ □ □

*Relays and amplifiers that boost a drone's built in energy protections.*

- Credit Cost: 5
- Keywords: Upgrade
- While this system is operable, this drone has +6 Shields.

---

**Sensor Array** - □ □ □ □ □

*A special module equipped with a range of common and specialty sensors and cameras.*

- Range: 6
- Credit Cost: 7
- Keywords: Upgrade
- When this drone enters range of a combatant, it can learn one of the following:
	- the target's Class
	- the target's Size
	- the target's Hull
	- the target's Speed
	- the target's Armor
	- the target's Shields
	- the remaining damage capacity of a particular weapon or other system of the target
	- the number of the target's systems that are not wrecked

---

**Gyro Thrusters** - □ □ □

*Small engine thrusters cover this drone on all sides, allowing it to glide through space with ease.*

- Credit Cost: 10
- Keywords: Upgrade
- This drone can move into any adjacent hex without needing to face it.

---

**Flickwhip Net** - □ □ □ □ □

**Haze Collapsers** - □ □ □ □ □

## Specialty Systems

### Dream of Ana

**Belt Buster (Front)** - □ □ □ □ □

*A shock wave emitter designed to clear a hole for the Dream of Ana to pass through a dense asteroid field.*

- Damage: 2d10 Energy
- Range: 3

---

**Long-Tail Engines (Rear)** - □ □ □

*The engine plumes of the Dreams of Ana are tuned for slower, stabler movement, burning in a long cone.*

- Damage: 4d4 Energy
- Range: 4
- Combatants that pass through this drone's rear line within range take this system's damage without a Drifter needing to spend any Drift dice.

---

**Pit Bays** - □ □ □ □

*A team of mechanics and munitions officers stand ready on-board the Dream of Ana to provide much needed repair and resupply during the heat of battle.*

- Range: 1
- When this drone is within range of an ally drone, it can repair the ally's systems or Hull for 2d6 points.

### Halsey Hauler

**Debris Deflectors (All)** - □ □ □ □

*Thickly plated railgun projectiles meant to protect the Halsey Hauler from random space junk.*

- Damage: 2d4 Ballistic
- Range: 5

---

**Detachable Fuel Tanks (All)** - □ □ □ □ □ □

*These massive canisters of refined rocket fuel may come in handy.*

- Damage: 2d8 Ballistic
- Range: 4
- Spawn 1 fuel tank within range. When a combatant enters a hex adjacent to a tank, the tank explodes and deals the listed damage. After exploding, the tank is removed.

---

**Engine Line** - □ □

*The Halsey Hauler uses its own fuel cargo to fly; a severe design flaw.*

- When this system is wrecked, the Halsey Hauler cannot move (can still maneuver). Repairing this system also repairs the Halsey Hauler's ability to move.
