# How to Build a Drone

The first step in building a drone from scratch is to pick your new drone's class. The class acts as a basic chassis upon which you will layer a multitude of upgrades and customizations to make your new drone work for your needs.

1. **Fighter** - The primary and most basic combat drone class. Highly versatile, quick, and cheap to build.
